HOW WE WORK WHY CHOOSE US? FIRM PROFILE OUR VALUE

Kickoff

First things first. Digital CineRiser will meet with your team to determine your unique goals and audiences, as well as your campaign budgets. We’ll want to know your competitors, your brand voice, and where you would like to position yourself in the digital space.

Evaluation

In the Evaluation phase, we get to work looking at your site, and evaluating your existing digital presence. We’ll look at site architecture and keyword rankings for SEO, review existing PPC or Social Media campaigns, and design a six month digital marketing strategy.

Plan Review

We’ll review our plan with your team to ensure it meets your goals and expectations, and adjust per your specific needs. We want you to know what we’ll be doing and when, based on your marketing priorities.

Implementation

Once you’ve given us the green light, we’ll get after it – building out your campaigns and getting work into the queue. Your Digital Sukoon project manager will be there to keep you in the loop.

You Talk, We Listen

As a digital marketing agency we strive to understand our client’s business goals first. Then all decisions are made with those goals in mind. A shiny new web site is worthless if it doesn’t help you reach your goals. You talk, we listen… then we throw in lots of ideas for improvement.

We Value Integrity

We believe in doing business with honesty, integrity and a sincere heart for others. We aren’t going to sell you something you don’t need. We won’t lie to get your business. We will treat you with kindness and respect. The way it should be (and far too often is not).

We Quantify Our Success

Visitor tracking allows us to quantify our success and see what is working and what is not. Monthly reports are emailed to you and full traffic data is available online anytime.

We Deliver On Time

We know that time is money in business, so we set realistic deadlines (for ourselves and for our clients) and stick to them. We communicate constantly with our clients to give them updates as we progress and get projects launched by the agreed launch date.
